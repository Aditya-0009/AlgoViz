About

Welcome to the Algorithm Visualiser! Our platform allows you to explore and understand various algorithms through interactive visualizations. Whether you're learning algorithms for the first time or are an experienced programmer looking to see how algorithms work in action, our tool provides a user-friendly and engaging experience.

Our visualiser includes a range of algorithms from sorting algorithms like Bubble Sort to graph algorithms like Dijkstra's Algorithm. Each algorithm comes with detailed explanations, complexity analysis, and interactive demos to help you grasp the underlying concepts.

We hope this tool helps you gain a deeper understanding of algorithms and fosters a passion for computer science. Happy learning!
